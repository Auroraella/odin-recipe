Introduction

Hi, welcome to my website introducing my favourite recipes. I am a girl who is passionate about cooking and baking. The story of cooking began since I was a little kid, after several times watching my parents cooking in the kitchen. I have a sweet tooth so I want to create a website with most of my favourite desserts. However, I am in love with many other dishes, so I will try my best to update various international recipes in the world. As a Vietnamese girl, I would like to share Vietnamese culture and tradition through the amazing recipes of Vietnamese cuisine in this website. In the future, after every time I make a dish successfully, I will update the website to share with everyone. Please continue follow my website to stay updated with how is Aurora going with her lovely recipes.
