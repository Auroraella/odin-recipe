Mango-Peach Smoothie

Description

The Mango-Peach Smoothie is a delicious and refreshing beverage that blends the sweetness of mangoes with the juicy freshness of peaches. It's perfect for a healthy breakfast or a midday snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and pitted
  • 2 ripe peaches, pitted
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the mango and peaches into chunks.
  2. Place the mango, peaches, banana, Greek yogurt, and orange juice in a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth. If desired, add honey for extra sweetness.
  4. Add ice cubes and blend again until you reach your preferred consistency.
  5. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
