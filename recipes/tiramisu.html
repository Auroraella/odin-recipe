Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert featuring layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cheese, dusted with cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk the egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale.
  2. Add mascarpone cheese to the mixture and blend until smooth.
  3. Combine coffee and coffee liqueur in a shallow dish.
  4. Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture briefly and arrange them in a serving dish.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  6. Repeat with another layer of soaked ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  7. Refrigerate the tiramisu for at least 4 hours.
  8. Before serving, dust with cocoa powder.
